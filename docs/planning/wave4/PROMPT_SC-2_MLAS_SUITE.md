## 1. Objective

Build the MLAS Suite to expose the full power of the MLAS Capability (CB-1), including configurable revenue sharing, multi-level attribution, abuse prevention, and flexible pricing per actor. The suite will provide the UI and APIs for partners and clients to manage their own affiliate and revenue-sharing ecosystems.

---

## 2. Scope & Requirements

### Mandatory Features:
1.  **Revenue Sharing Configuration:** UI and API for defining complex revenue sharing rules.
2.  **Multi-Level Attribution:** Track and attribute sales and commissions across multiple levels of affiliates.
3.  **Abuse Prevention:** Implement mechanisms to detect and prevent fraudulent activity.
4.  **Flexible Pricing:** Allow partners and clients to set their own pricing models for their affiliates.
