# FOUNDER LOCK-IN DIRECTIVE

**Document Type:** Founder Directive  
**Authority:** Founder (WebWaka)  
**Status:** EFFECTIVE IMMEDIATELY  
**Scope:** All Manus Agents, Present and Future  
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2026

---

## 1. Declaration of Finality

Effective immediately, the following governance and identity architecture for the WebWaka Agentic Software Factory is hereby **LOCKED IN** as institutional infrastructure:

- The GitHub Organization **webwaka-factory**
- The **Hybrid GitHub Teams + Bot Model**
- Enforced **CODEOWNERS**
- Enforced **Branch Protection Rules**
- **Team-based accountability and review authority**

This architecture is **non-experimental, non-provisional, and non-negotiable**.

---

## 2. Non-Reversibility Clause

No Manus agent may:

- Bypass or weaken CODEOWNERS
- Merge to protected branches without required team approval
- Reassign review authority outside designated teams
- Introduce alternative identity systems
- Propose reverting to single-account attribution

**Without explicit, written Founder authorization.**

Any deviation constitutes a governance incident.

---

## 3. Authority Boundary Clarification

- **Founder** remains the sole human decision authority
- **Teams** represent institutional agent roles, not individuals
- **GitHub enforcement mechanisms** are the source of truth
- **Social conventions, comments, or intent** do not override technical enforcement

---

## 4. Applicability

This directive applies to:

- All current Manus agents
- All future Manus agents
- All repositories under webwaka-factory
- All workflows, automations, and CI/CD processes

---

## 5. Enforcement

The Governance & Compliance Agent is hereby mandated to:

- Monitor compliance continuously
- Flag any violation immediately
- Escalate unresolved incidents directly to the Founder

---

## 6. Status

**Status:** ACTIVE & ENFORCEABLE  
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2026

This directive establishes the permanent governance substrate of the WebWaka Agentic Software Factory.
